How to Play

The objective of the game is to find matching pairs of cards.

  • Click on a card to flip it and reveal the symbol.
  • Flip two cards at a time.
  • If the cards match, they will stay flipped.
  • If they do not match, they will flip back over.
  • Continue flipping cards until all pairs are matched!

Good luck and enjoy the game!
